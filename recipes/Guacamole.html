Guacamole

this recipe is a recipe for the best homemeade Guacamole. this Guacamole is quick and easy to make and most of all tasty

Guacamole recipe

Ingrediets

  • 3 avocadoes-peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper(optional)

Steps

  1. grab a medium bowl
  2. mash together the avocados
  3. add in the 1 juiced lime
  4. add in the teaspoon of salt
  5. mix in the onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic
  6. stir in cayenne pepper
  7. refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor
  8. serve immediately
